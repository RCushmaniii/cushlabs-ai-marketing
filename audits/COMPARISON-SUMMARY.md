## What NYET Does Better (Lessons for CushLabs)

### 1. Social Proof (+32 conversion delta)
NYET has 15 named testimonials from C-suite executives at recognizable companies (Driscoll's, CEVA Logistics, Continental, Infosys). CushLabs has zero. This single difference accounts for the largest scoring gap across all categories.

**Action for CushLabs:** Collect 3-5 testimonials from past project clients. Even anonymized quotes with industry context would dramatically close this gap.

### 2. Content Depth (+24 content delta)
NYET has an active blog targeting specific professional verticals (Tech English, Logistics English), a 90-second assessment tool, an AI chatbot, and a resources page. CushLabs has no blog, no content marketing, and no lead magnet.

**Action for CushLabs:** Launch bi-weekly content targeting specific AI implementation verticals. Each of the 27 portfolio projects is an untapped content asset.

### 3. Lead Capture Mechanisms (+37 growth delta)
NYET offers multiple entry points: free assessment, AI chatbot, blog, consultation booking, WhatsApp. CushLabs offers only one: book a consultation call.

**Action for CushLabs:** Add at least one lower-commitment capture mechanism (AI readiness checklist, email newsletter, free resource download).

### 4. Category Creation (+23 competitive delta)
NYET defines its own micro-category: "Executive English Performance Coaching — not ESL." This immediately differentiates from every marketplace tutor and language school. CushLabs positions as "AI consulting" — a crowded, undifferentiated category.

**Action for CushLabs:** Define a specific micro-category. "Boutique AI Implementation for Growing Businesses" or "The Solo AI Partner for Companies Between 5-200 Employees" — then own it.

## What CushLabs Does Better (Lessons for NYET)

### 1. Pricing Transparency
CushLabs publishes clear fixed-price ranges ($1,500-$15,000+) with service-specific tiers and a "not a good fit" qualifier. This is a competitive advantage in consulting where most competitors hide pricing.

**Lesson for NYET:** NYET's pricing is transparent but dramatically underpriced. The $25/session price point undermines the executive positioning.

### 2. Portfolio Depth
CushLabs has 27 live portfolio projects with working demos — far more than most AI consultancies show. The demos prove technical capability in a way that descriptions cannot.

**Lesson for NYET:** Consider adding before/after audio samples or live demo links to show communication transformation in action.
